About

We lead with purpose and serve with heart.

Leo Club of Ananda College is a community of driven young leaders committed to service, development, and positive change—on campus and beyond under Leo District 306 D7.

Vision

To inspire and empower compassionate leaders.

The vision of Leo Club of Ananda College is to be recognized as a leading youth organization, known for its exceptional commitment to service and its dedication to developing the next generation of leaders. We envision a world where young people are empowered to take ownership of their communities and to create meaningful change through service, leadership, and personal growth. We strive to create a culture of service that is sustainable, impactful, and inspiring, and to build a community of like-minded individuals who share our vision and values.

Mission

Serving with purpose, leading with heart.

The mission of Leo Club of Ananda College is to empower youth leaders to make a positive impact in their community through service, leadership, and personal growth. We strive to develop our members' leadership skills, encourage a culture of service, and create opportunities for personal and professional development. Our goal is to inspire a new generation of leaders who are committed to making a difference in the world, and to create a sustainable impact that leaves a lasting legacy. With a strong sense of purpose and a deep commitment to service, we aim to build a better future for ourselves and for those around us.
